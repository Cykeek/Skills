# Persuasion Frameworks for SaaS

## PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

*   **Problem**: "Struggling to track your SaaS metrics?"
*   **Agitate**: "It’s costing you time, money, and growth."
*   **Solution**: "Our dashboard gives you real-time insights in one click."

## AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

*   **Attention**: "Most dashboards are slow. Ours is instant."
*   **Interest**: "See your data update in real time."
*   **Desire**: "Join 1,000+ teams who’ve switched."
*   **Action**: "[Try it free] →"
