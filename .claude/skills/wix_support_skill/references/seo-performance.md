## Promotion & Marketing (Enhanced)

### SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
**Foundation:**
- **SEO Setup Checklist:** Guided wizard for basic site SEO configuration
- **Page-Level SEO:** Individual control over title tags, meta descriptions, URLs
- **Site-Level SEO:** Global settings for homepage, default social images, language

**Advanced SEO Features:**
- **Structured Data/Schema Markup:** Automatic generation for:
  - Business information (LocalBusiness schema)
  - Blog posts/articles (Article schema)
  - Products (Product schema - requires Wix Stores)
  - Events (Event schema - requires Wix Events)
  - FAQ pages (FAQPage schema)
  - Reviews (Review schema)
  - Jobs (JobPosting schema)
  - Recipes (Recipe schema)
  - Videos (VideoObject schema)
- **Custom Schema Injection:** Via Header/Footer code or Velo (wix-seo API)
- **URL Management:** Custom URLs, 301 redirects, canonical tags
- **Indexing Control:** Per-page "Allow search engines to index" toggle
- **Mobile SEO:** Automatic mobile optimization; AMP not supported natively

**SEO Tools & Analytics:**
- **SEO Dashboard (2024+):** Keyword ranking tracking, competitor analysis
- **Search Insights:** See what queries bring users to your site (via GSC integration)
- **Content Gap Analysis:** Identify topics competitors rank for that you don't
- **Backlink Monitoring:** Track referring domains and anchor text distribution
- **Site Audit:** Technical SEO checks for crawlability, speed, mobile usability
- **Keyword Research Tool:** Suggest relevant terms with volume/difficulty metrics

**SEO Best Practices:**
1. **Keyword Research:** Target mix of high-volume and long-tail terms
2. **On-Page Optimization:** Primary keyword in title, first 100 words, headings
3. **Content Quality:** Comprehensive, original content that satisfies user intent
4. **Internal Linking:** Connect related content with descriptive anchor text
5. **Image Optimization:** Descriptive filenames, alt text, compressed file sizes
6. **Technical Health:** Fix crawl errors, improve site speed, ensure mobile usability
7. **Local SEO:** Consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across directories; Google Business Profile
8. **Content Freshness:** Regularly update evergreen content; publish new material consistently
9. **User Experience:** Clear navigation, fast loading, intuitive design reduces bounce rate
10. **Link Building:** Earn links through valuable content, partnerships, guest posting

**Common SEO Issues & Solutions:**
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **Pages not indexed** | Check "Allow search engines" is ON; submit URL via GSC; ensure no crawl blocks |
| **Rankings dropping suddenly** | Check for algorithm updates; review recent site changes; analyze backlink loss |
| **Thin content warnings** | Expand short pages with valuable information; consolidate related thin pages |
| **Duplicate content** | Use canonical tags; parameter handling; consolidate similar pages |
| **Slow loading times** | Optimize images; enable browser caching; reduce render-blocking resources |
| **Mobile usability errors** | Fix touch element spacing; use legible font sizes; avoid incompatible plugins |
| **Keyword cannibalization** | Differentiate page topics; consolidate overlapping content; use internal linking |
| **Local rankings not appearing** | Verify GMB listing; ensure NAP consistency; get local citations and reviews |

### Marketing Integrations & Tracking

#### Analytics & Measurement
**Built-in Analytics:**
- **Site Visits:** Total visits, unique visitors, new vs returning
- **Traffic Sources:** Direct, search (organic/paid), social, referral, email
- **Geographic Data:** Visitor countries, cities, languages
- **Device Breakdown:** Desktop vs mobile vs tablet; operating systems
- **Page Performance:** Most visited pages, average time on page, bounce rate
- **Conversion Tracking:** Goals for form submissions, button clicks, page visits
- **E-commerce Metrics:** Revenue, conversion rate, average order value, top products

**Marketing Integrations:**
- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4):** Enhanced e-commerce, event-based modeling
  - Setup: Measurement ID in Marketing Integrations > Analytics
  - Features: Custom events, user properties, funnel analysis, predictive metrics
- **Facebook/Meta Pixel:** Conversion tracking, retargeting, lookalike audiences
  - Standard events: PageView, ViewContent, AddToCart, Purchase, Lead
  - Custom events for specific user actions
- **Google Tag Manager (GTM):** Container-based tag management
  - Deploy marketing and analytics tags without touching site code
  - Version control, debugging environment, triggers based on user behavior
- **TikTok Pixel:** Track views, add to cart, purchases for TikTok ad campaigns
- **Pinterest Tag:** Monitor visits, signups, checkouts for Pinterest advertising
- **Snapchat Pixel:** Track swipe ups, app installs, purchases for Snapchat Ads
- **LinkedIn Insight Tag:** Monitor visits, conversions, retarget for LinkedIn Ads

**UTM Parameters & Campaign Tracking:**
- Manual tagging of URLs for custom campaign tracking
- Standard utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_term, utm_content
- Visible in analytics under "Campaigns" or custom reports
- Essential for measuring effectiveness of email, social, paid advertising

1. **List Building:** Use double opt-in; offer valuable lead magnets; clear value proposition
2. **Segmentation:** Send relevant content to specific audience segments
3. **Subject Lines:** Keep under 50 characters; create curiosity; avoid spam triggers
4. **Preheader Text:** Complement subject line; provide additional incentive to open
5. **Mobile Optimization:** Single-column layout; large touch targets; readable fonts
6. **Clear CTA:** One primary action per email; button-style links; clear benefit
7. **Send Time Optimization:** Test different days/times; consider audience time zones
8. **Frequency Management:** Balance staying top-of-mind with avoiding fatigue
9. **List Hygiene:** Regularly remove inactive subscribers; handle bounces/complaints
10. **Testing:** Always send test emails; check rendering across clients/devices

#### Paid Advertising (PPC)
**Google Ads Integration:**
- **Search Campaigns:** Text ads in Google search results
- **Display Campaigns:** Banner ads across Google Display Network
- **Shopping Campaigns:** Product listings in Google Shopping (requires product feed)
- **Video Campaigns:** Ads on YouTube and video partner sites
- **App Campaigns:** Promote mobile apps across Google's properties
- **Local Campaigns:** Drive physical store location visits (for brick-and-mortar)

**Facebook/Meta Ads:**
- **Awareness:** Brand reach, video views, store traffic
- **Consideration:** Traffic, engagement, app installs, lead generation
- **Conversion:** Conversions, catalog sales, messages
- **Ad Formats:** Image, video, carousel, collection, slideshow, lead ads
- **Placement Options:** Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Stories, Marketplace, Audience Network
- **Targeting:** Demographics, interests, behaviors, connections, custom audiences, lookalikes

**Microsoft Advertising (Bing Ads):**
- Similar campaign types to Google Ads
- Often lower CPC; different demographic (older, higher income on average)
- Import campaigns directly from Google Ads for efficiency

**Advertising Best Practices:**
1. **Clear Objectives:** Define campaign goal (awareness, leads, sales) before launching
2. **Audience Targeting:** Start broad, then refine based on performance data
3. **Compelling Creative:** High-quality images/videos; clear value proposition; strong CTA
4. **Landing Page Match:** Ensure ad message aligns with landing page content
5. **Conversion Tracking:** Implement proper tracking to measure true ROI
6. **Budget Allocation:** Start test budgets; scale winning campaigns; pause losers
7. **Ad Rotation:** Test multiple ad variations; use data to inform creative decisions
8. **Bid Strategy:** Choose appropriate strategy (maximize conversions, target CPA, etc.)
9. **Ad Schedule:** Run ads during peak business hours or when audience is most active
10. **Landing Page Optimization:** Ensure fast loading, clear navigation, minimal distractions

**Tracking & Attribution:**
- **View-Through Conversions:** Count conversions where user saw ad but didn't click
- **Assisted Conversions:** Credit touchpoints that contributed to conversion path
- **Data-Driven Attribution:** Use algorithmic models to assign credit across touchpoints
- **Offline Conversions:** Import in-store or phone sales to connect online ads to offline results
- **Customer Lifetime Value (LVC):** Factor in repeat purchases when evaluating campaign profitability

#### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)
**Landing Page Optimization:**
- **Message Match:** Ensure ad/email content aligns with landing page headline
- **Clear Value Proposition:** State primary benefit above the fold
- **Minimal Distractions:** Remove navigation; focus on single conversion goal
- **Trust Elements:** Testimonials, security badges, guarantees, client logos
- **Form Optimization:** Minimize fields; use progressive profiling; clear error messages
- **Social Proof:** Show recent activity; customer counts; rating distributions
- **Scarcity/Urgency:** Limited time offers; low stock indicators; countdown timers
- **Exit Intent:** Pop-ups offering discount or resource when user shows exit behavior
- **Mobile-First Design:** Prioritize mobile experience; test thoroughly on actual devices

**A/B Testing Framework:**
- **Test One Variable:** Headline, image, CTA button, form length, layout
- **Statistical Significance:** Ensure sufficient sample size before declaring winner
- **Test Duration:** Run for full business cycles (typically 1-2 weeks)
- **Segmentation:** Analyze results by traffic source, device type, new vs returning visitors
- **Implementation:** Roll out winning variation; document learnings for future tests
- **Continuous Improvement:** Always be testing; small improvements compound over time

**Common CRO Tests:**
1. **Headline Variations:** Benefit-focused vs feature-focused vs question-based
2. **CTA Button Text:** "Get Started" vs "Learn More" vs "Claim Your Offer"
3. **Form Fields:** Reducing number of required fields vs adding qualifying questions
4. **Image Types:** Product photos vs lifestyle images vs videos vs illustrations
5. **Page Length:** Short and focused vs long-form with detailed information
6. **Price Presentation:** Annual vs monthly pricing; showing savings; tiered options
7. **Trust Placement:** Testimonials near CTA; security badges near payment fields
8. **Navigation:** Remove header/footer vs simplified nav vs full site navigation
9. **Offer Type:** Percentage discount vs dollar amount; free gift vs free shipping
10. **Page Load:** Impact of improving load time on conversion rates (even 1s matters)

#### Affiliate & Referral Programs
**Referral Program Setup:**
- **Reward Structure:** Fixed amount, percentage discount, store credit, free product
- **Referral Mechanism:** Unique links, coupon codes, or both
- **Tracking:** Cookies or account-based attribution for referral source
- **Reward Timing:** Immediate upon referral action vs after purchase completion
- **Fraud Prevention:** Limit self-referrals; minimum purchase requirements; manual review tiers
- **Promotion Channels:** Email, social media, website banners, customer service scripts

**Affiliate Marketing (Advanced):**
- **Network Selection:** ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, Impact, Rakuten, Amazon Associates
- **Recruitment:** Target relevant blogs, influencers, coupon sites, comparison engines
- **Creative Assets:** Provide banners, text links, product feeds, exclusive coupon codes
- **Commission Structure:** Percentage of sale; fixed amount; tiered based on volume
- **Cookie Duration:** Typically 7-30 days; longer for considered purchases
- **Policy Guidelines:** Trademark bidding restrictions; content guidelines; disclosure requirements
- **Performance Monitoring:** Track clicks, conversions, revenue, ROI by affiliate/publisher
- **Payment Processing:** Automatic vs manual; minimum thresholds; tax documentation
