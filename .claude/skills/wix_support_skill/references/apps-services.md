## Wix Account & Billing Management

### Account Settings
**Path:** Dashboard → Settings → Account & Billing (or via profile icon)

**Manageable Items:**
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Login credentials and security settings
- Notification preferences
- Language and regional settings
- Connected apps and integrations
- Collaborator access and permissions

**Common Tasks:**
- Updating contact information
- Changing password or enabling two-factor authentication
- Managing email notifications for store orders, form submissions, etc.
- Setting primary language for dashboard and editor

### Subscription & Billing Management
**Viewing Current Plan:**
Dashboard → Settings → Subscription & Billing

**Plan Details Displayed:**
- Current plan name and renewal date
- Monthly/annual cost and payment method
- Included features (storage, bandwidth, apps)
- Number of connected domains
- Contributors/collaborators allowed

**Changing Plans:**
- **Upgrading:** Available anytime; prorated charge for remainder of billing cycle
- **Downgrading:** Takes effect at next billing cycle; may lose access to premium features
- **Cancelling:** Site reverts to Free plan at end of billing period; Wix ads may return

### Payment Methods
**Managing Payment Information:**
Dashboard → Settings → Payment Methods

**Supported Methods:**
- Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal (in supported regions)
- Bank transfer/ACH (for certain business accounts)
- Local payment methods (varies by country)

**Updating Payment Info:**
- Add new payment method
- Set as primary
- Remove expired or unused methods
- Update billing address

### Invoices & Payment History
**Accessing Invoices:**
Dashboard → Settings → Billing History

**Invoice Information:**
- Invoice number and date
- Billing period covered
- Itemized charges (subscriptions, add-ons, overages)
- Taxes applied
- Total amount paid
- Payment status and method

**Actions Available:**
- Download PDF invoice
- View payment receipt
- Report issues with specific charges
- Request VAT/GST invoices for business accounting

### Troubleshooting Billing Issues

#### Common Problems & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| **Payment declined** | Verify card details, sufficient funds, international transaction permissions; try alternative payment method |
| **Unexpected charge** | Review billing history for subscriptions/apps; check for annual vs monthly billing confusion; contact support if unrecognized |
| **Failed to renew** | Update payment method before due to expired card; ensure sufficient funds; check bank restrictions on recurring payments |
| **Charged after cancellation** | Verify cancellation was completed before billing cycle end; refunds typically processed within 5-10 business days |
| **Tax charges incorrect** | Confirm business address in account settings; VAT/GST ID may be required for tax-exempt status in some regions |
| **Can't access premium features** | Confirm payment was successful; check if downgraded accidentally; allow up to 24 hours for provisioning after payment |

#### Refund Policies
- **Annual plans:** Refundable within 14 days of payment (pro-rated after)
- **Monthly plans:** Generally non-refundable for current month; cancellation prevents future charges
- **Add-ons/apps:** Refund eligibility varies by provider; check individual terms
- **Domain purchases:** Typically non-refundable after registration; some TLDs have grace periods
- **Wix Arena (ticketed events):** Refunds subject to event organizer policy

#### Preventing Service Interruption
- Set payment method to auto-renew
- Keep payment information current (especially expiration dates)
- Monitor email for payment failure notifications
- Maintain sufficient balance/credit limit
- Consider setting up backup payment method

### Account Security
**Best Practices:**
- Use unique, strong password not used elsewhere
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) via authenticator app
- Regularly review connected apps and revoke unused permissions
- Monitor login activity for unfamiliar locations/devices
- Keep recovery email and phone number current

**Sign-In Issues:**
- **Forgot password:** Use "Forgot password?" link on login page
- **Account locked:** Typically due to multiple failed attempts; wait 15-30 minutes or use account recovery
- **Email not received:** Check spam/junk folder; verify correct email associated with account
- **Two-factor issues:** Use backup codes if authenticator app inaccessible

### Special Account Types
**Team/Collaborator Accounts:**
- Invited via Dashboard → Settings → Roles & Permissions
- Permissions customized per role (Editor, Admin, CMS Editor, etc.)
- No access to billing/account settings unless granted Admin role
- Use own Wix credentials to access invited sites

**Enterprise Accounts:**
- Dedicated account management
- Consolidated billing for multiple sites
- Advanced security controls (SSO, SAML)
- Custom contract terms and SLAs

**Nonprofit/Educational Discounts:**
- Available through application process
- Requires verification of eligibility status
- Discounts apply to specific plans; contact sales for details
